A Quick Intro

He still faces college and many other life events standing ahead of him. With the help of his friends, family, and many other influential people in his life, he is ready to face any obstacle in his way.


Early Life

Alex Michael Dominguez was born in Alamogordo, New Mexico, which was a small town near the Trinity Test site where the first nuclear bomb was tested and detonated. Today, it has a population of nearly 40,000 people and is located near White Sands Nat'l Monument and an hour north of El Paso, Texas. The city is located in South-Central New Mexico.

Childhood

Alex grew up always on the go and has traveled to many different places. He always had to adapt and change to new environments and new people. For now, he sees California and New Mexico as his homes.

  • Currently
